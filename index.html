Issues Released!

Dreams ~ Sapne

In this issue of Biscuit Digest, dreams take center stage as a realm where imagination and reality blur. Through a collection of art we delve into the mysterious world of dreams—those vivid, surreal experiences that inspire, and provoke us. From explorations of dream symbolism and the science of sleep to the creative bursts that arise from nocturnal visions, this issue invites readers to reflect on the hidden landscapes of their minds.

Baaghi ~ Rebellion

Issue Three of Biscuit Digest dives into the theme of Baaghi, or rebellion—a powerful force that challenge norms and ignites change. This issue captures the spirit of defiance through bold art, striking photography, thought-provoking essays, and poetry that celebrates resistance in all its forms. From personal stories of standing against societal expectations to reflections on revolutionary movements that shaped history, the content explores what it means to be a rebel today. It’s a tribute to those who dare to question, disrupt, and reimagine the status quo, inviting readers to find their own inner baaghi.
